Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to QuickServ. We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy describes how we collect, use, and share your personal information when you use our mobile application and services.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information that you provide directly to us, including:

  • Personal Identifiers: Name, phone number, and OTP authentication data when you register.
  • Dual Profile System: Users can simultaneously hold "Customer" and "Professional/Provider" accounts. We collect specific data based on the active role.
  • Provider Information: Professional photos, service category, subscriptions, and business descriptions (if acting as a Professional).
  • Location Data: We collect precise or approximate location information to connect customers with nearby service providers.
  • Usage Data: Information about your bookings, chat history, and platform interactions.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain our marketplace services.
  • Process and manage service bookings.
  • Facilitate communication between providers and customers.
  • Send technical notices, updates, and support messages.
  • Improve and personalize the user experience.

4. Real-time Communication

Our application includes real-time chat features. Messages exchanged between users are stored on our secure servers to provide chat history and ensure a seamless service experience.

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data. However, please note that no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure.

6. Your Rights & Data Deletion

You have full control over your personal data:

  • Granular Profile Deletion: Because of our Dual Profile System, you can choose to permanently delete one side of your account (e.g., Delete Professional Profile) while retaining the other (e.g., Customer Profile). This will securely wipe all business data, gallery, and related provider bookings.
  • Full Account Deletion: You can request a total account wipe from the Settings menu. This will permanently erase your phone number login, both profiles, and all historical data from our servers.
